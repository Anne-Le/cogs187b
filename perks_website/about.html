About Us

We're a fun, family of coffee lovers!

Perks Coffee Shop is a unique café located in the UCSD Bookstore next to Imprints. Upon entering you will be welcomed by the warmth of our talented Baristas and Perky the Squirrel. The Perks environment is also a perfect study spot where we offer a range of seats from individual study tables, group tables, and a cozy corner equipped with a fireplace for de-stress.

We're Ethical

Perks brews and serves only Barefoot Coffee Roasters coffee. All of Barefoot coffees are direct trade. Our pastries, salads, and sandwiches are prepared fresh locally. We guarantee the finest flavors, quality ingredients, and an unparalleled customer service experience.


We love our customers!

Perky the Squirrel offers customers a chance to win a variety of prizes throughout the year through art contests as a way to say “thank you” to all of our loyal customers!

Whether it’s breakfast, lunch or a mid-day gathering, include a scrumptious selection of muffins, cakes, tarts, cookies and bars. Perks is your ideal partner for every size meeting and department get together.
