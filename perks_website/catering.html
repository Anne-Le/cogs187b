Catering Services

Let us take care of your catering needs!

Whether it’s breakfast, lunch or a mid-day gathering, include a scrumptious selection of muffins, cakes, tarts, cookies and bars. Perks is your ideal partner for every size meeting and department get together.


Commonly Asked Questions

How far in advance do I need to place my order?

If simply ordering coffee, please allow a minimum of 60 minutes so we can brew the freshest coffee for you!

If placing a food order, for lunch items please place your order 72 hours in advance. For breakfast items simply place your order before 6pm for next day catering.

What is included with catering orders?

Free of charge all catering orders will include the appropriate utensils, plating, napkins, condiments, and other items needed for set up. With coffee orders come the previous items as well as cups, cream, sugars, stirrers, as well as disposable traveler boxes.

All delivery orders can be set up by Perks staff!

I want to place a catering order, what forms of payment do you accept?

Perks accepts all major credit cards, Triton Cash, student accounts and UCSD recharge accounts.

Do catering services include delivery or do I need to pick up in store?

Perks can deliver for a $5 fee or for free with orders over $50. You are always free to pick up in store!
