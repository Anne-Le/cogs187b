Commonly Asked Questions

Do you offer extended work hours during finals week?
Unfortunately, we are unable to extend our working hours during finals week. However, we are open from 7am to 7pm every weekday and from noon to 5pm on weekends.

I want to place a catering order, what forms of payment do you accept?
Perks accepts UCSD recharge accounts, Triton Cash, student accounts, and all forms of major credit cards as payment methods.

Do catering services include delivery or do I need to pick up in store?
Perks can deliver for a $5 fee or for free with orders over $50. You also have the option to pick up in store for free always!
